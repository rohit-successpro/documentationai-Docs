## Overview

Team collaboration boosts productivity.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Navigate to your project page in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Team tab">
    Click the <b>Team</b> tab at the top menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invites">
    ````
    Enter email address and click 'Invite'.
    Invited users will receive an email to join.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
